Harry and Voldy are BFFs.

They are both gifted wizards whose only wish is to win the UK's greatest tournament of all - Britain's got talent.

One sunny afternoon they stroll together to the forbidden forest, where they can practice their show and try to beat each other while entertaining Simon "The Sorcerer" Cowel.

Choose your player and let's get it on!

Voldy has 100 health and slow but powerful spells.
He moves with these keys:

  • W
  • E
  • R
  • S
  • F
  • X
  • C
  • V
- Use the "1" key to cast "Avada-Kedavra" - a dark spell that causes 100 damage.

- Use the "2" key to cast "Crucio" - a dark spell that causes 50 damage and paralyzes Harry for 2 seconds.

- Use the "3" key to cast "Imperio" - a dark spell that causes 35 damage and moves helpless Harry towards the center of the screen for 3 seconds.

Harry has 100 health and faster but less powerful spells.
He moves with the keypad:

  • 7
  • 8
  • 9
  • 4
  • 6
  • 1
  • 2
  • 3
- Use the "\" key to cast "Expelliarmus" - a spell that causes 35 damage and prevents Voldy from casting any spells for 4 seconds. That's a lot of time.

- Use the "]" key to cast "Stupefy" - a spell that causes 35 damage and paralyzes Voldy for 2 seconds.

- Use the "[" key to cast "Sectum-Sempra" - a dark spell that causes 50 damage.
